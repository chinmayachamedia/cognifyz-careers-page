// about-the-program

Not a coffee-run internship. A build-real-things internship.

The Cognifyz Web Developer Internship is built for people who learn by shipping. You'll work inside our actual development pipeline — writing components, fixing bugs, reviewing pull requests, and deploying features that real users touch.

You won't be handed a syllabus and left alone. Every intern is paired with a senior developer who reviews your code, unblocks you when you're stuck, and helps you think like an engineer — not just someone who can make a button turn blue.
